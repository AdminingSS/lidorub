захватывающее представление

Добро пожаловать в изумительный мир Paris Merveilles – новое ревю знаменитого Франко Драгоне. Добро пожаловать в красочную феерию, посвященную Парижу. Мерцающие огни, разноцветные пышные перья и самые красивые девушки на свете перенесут Вас в мир чувственности и элегантности, где исполняются самые сокровенные желания. Добро пожаловать в мечту, ставшую явью благодаря небывалым техническим достижениям.

Подробнее

Каждый вечер талантливые артисты сменяют друг друга на сцене Лидо: захватывающие выступления акробатов и наших невероятных фигуристов, к которым присоединятся самые гламурные и элегантные парижские танцовщицы «Блюбэль Герлз»!

Уникальное место

При входе в кабаре Лидо в Париже нас охватывает такое же изумление и восхищение, как когда мы открываем шкатулку с сокровищами. Роскошные люстры с мириадами подвесок, золотистая мозаика, завораживающая своими переливами... Пройдя по коридору цвета ночного неба, мы попадаем в недавно обновленный панорамный зрительный зал вместимостью более 1 100 человек. Внимание! Спектакль начинается...

На Елисейских Полях всегда кипит жизнь. Эта истинно парижская авеню, воплощающая в себе искусство жить по-французски, входит в число красивейших улиц в мире.

Synopsis


ACT I
Scene one

The terrace of castle is filled with joy and merriment - Prince Siegfried is celebrating his coming of age, his courtiers congratulate him while his mischievous jester entertains the assembled guests. The servants announce the arrival of his mother the queen, she presents her son with a crossbow for the hunt. The queen reminds her son that at tomorrow coming of age ball he will have to choose a bride. Night is fast approaching and the guests take leave, Siegfried is finally alone with his thoughts and premonitions of what the future may bring. In the twilight a flock of swans pass over and he rushes to the lake. 

Scene two

At the lake in the heart of the forest, the swans having stepped ashore turn into beautiful young girls Siegfried about to draw his crossbow stops fascinated by their beauty. Odette the swan princess is startled by Siegfrieds presence, he assures her no harm will come to her. Odette tells him of her terrible plight. Tells him of her high birth and how she has fallen under the spell of be a swan and only in the hours of darkness to assume her human form. She tells him she is bound for eternity by the spell unless an unblemished youth swears eternal fidelity and marries her. Siegfried realizes his destiny has changed. Day is breaking, the swans are again swimming on the lake, Odette bids a sad farewell, Siegfried is left desolate.

ACT II
Scene Three

The coming of age ball commences at the castle. The young noblewomen whom the Queen has chosen as prospective brides are presented to Prince Siegfried, he shows no interest thinking only of Odette. The entertainment and merrymaking continue until a fanfare announces the arrival of more guests. Rothbart disguised as a knight sweeps in with his daughter Odille, Siegfried is dazzled by her beauty and her resemblance to Odette, swears his love and fidelity..... Rothbart the sorcerer triumphs in his deception. Siegfried horrified at what he has done rushes from the ball and into the night.

ACT III
Scene four

At the lakeside Odette tells her fellow swans of Rothbarts devilish trickery. Siegfried fleeing from the ball arrives at the shore begging Odette’s forgiveness and telling of his undying love for her. Rothbart appears and he and his black swans battle with Siegfried and the forces of good until the light of the dawn breaks and the forces of evil are vanquished. The rising sun gives birth to new life, happiness and love.

The Alexandrinsky Theatre is one of the oldest national theatres in Russia. From the very beginning of its existence this Theatre became one of the empire theatres and the cradle of the dramatic art. Second name -The Russian State Pushkin Academy DramaTheatre. The troupe of the Theatre was created by the order of empress Elizabeth on August 30, 1756, exactly this date is considered to be the birthday of Russian national theatre. In 1832 the Theatre was named in honor of Nicolas I’s wife Alexandra Feodorovna.  In 2005 the theatre was closed to the reconstruction and was opened on a date of celebration the 250 anniversary of the theatre, on August 30, 2006. The Theatre returned its historical look and was improved in technical point. In 2012 while celebration the 180 anniversary of the Theatre building, the Theatre educational complex “The New stage of Alexandrinsky theatre” was inaugurated. 

Alexandrinsky Theatre, pl. Ostrovskogo, 6, St Petersburg, 191011

About the building

Nice yellow classical building was created by outstanding Italian architect Carlos Rossi. Being the Empire theatre it was created monumental and luxury like usual, decorated with columns and a bronze sculpture on the top. Rossi chose the Empire stile, using his favorite colors: yellow and white. Inside you’ll see a lot of red velvet, molding and a wonderful curtain that covers the stage. The Theatre is famous for its corridors and numerous stairs, so be careful not to be lost in them. 

History of Alexandrinsky Theatre

Apart from famous Russian dramatic place like “Uncle Vanya”, “The Seagul” by Chekhov, “A Sunstroke” by Bunin, “The Reviser”, “Marriage” by Gogol, you can enjoy the festival plays of foreign authors: a choreography play “Snow White”, “Macbeth”, “The Marriage of Figaro”, “War and Peace”, etc. From 2005 V.V. Fokin is the director of the Theatre, the laureate of four Russian state awards and the president of the Meyerhold cultural theatre center. Visiting the Alexandrinsky Theatre you need to follow the dress code, black tie for men and evening dresses for women are not really the must, but you’d better keep formal style. During the cold season(autumn, winter, early spring) remember about a possible snow and rain outside, that’s why don’t forget to take shoes to change and to leave your coat and hat in the cloakroom. The Theatre is situated in the middle of the Nevsky prospect at Ostrovskogo Square, one of the nicest squares in Saint Petersburg, 3 minutes walk from Nevsky prospect. It’s very easy to get there by walking along the beautiful streets of Saint Petersburg, by underground (station Nevsky prospect- Gostiny dvor) or by taxi if your hotel is situated far away from the city center. Beside we’ll gladly organize a transfer for you from the hotel or from your cruise ship. The place is worth visiting every time of the year. In June during the marvelous White Nights our city gladly opens its doors for a lot of festivals. Every year the Theatre organize a special festival that is called “Aleksandrinsky” this year it take place in September - October, so people have an opportunity to see a lot of modern plays of Russian and foreign masters. Generally the time May-July is considered the most romantic and nice time for visiting theatres because apart from the precious performance you can enjoy wonderful White Nights of Saint Petersburg!
