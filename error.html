Paris
Merveilles

Paris Merveilles, the Lido’s current show, has been created and is directed by Franco Dragone.

Unique and sensational choreography has been created by Benoit Swan Pouffer, a world-renowned choreographer. This show is the Parisian artistic event.

Join our artists for an enriching and unforgettable experience.

Become a Bluebell Girl or Lido Boy Dancer!

We expect you to meet the following criteria:

  • Dance training: a high level of ballet and modern jazz is required

  • Female dancers: minimum height of 175 cm

  • Male dancers: minimum height of 183 cm

  • Elegant figure for women

  • Finely muscular figure for men

  • Excellent stage presence

Do you meet these criteria?

Don’t hesitate to send your CV and photos to:

casting@lido.fr
